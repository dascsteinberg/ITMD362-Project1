Welcome to Roll Call

Roll call is a support community for veterans. We provide a way for people to reach out to and meet with other fellow veterans. Whether that be for help with benefits, a job fair, or just grabbing a drink at a local bar. We will help plan events and allow you to set up your own meet-ups. There will even be an interactive map showing local events and establishments that are veteran friendly. Sign up today to join a caring support community that's for veterans, by veterans.
